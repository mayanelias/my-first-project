About Tel-aviv hotel



Situated a short stroll from Tel-aviv Center Railway Station,
Tel-aviv hotel provides guests with a convenient base while visiting Tel-aviv.
It also offers free Wi-Fi,a swimming pool and a terrace.
Those staying at the property can make the most of the property's gardens.
Apartments feature modern features, such as mini bars and flat-screen TVs.
Each provide tea and coffee making facilities, an in-room closet and a private bathroom with a shower.
Tel-aviv hotel is ideally located for discovering Tel-aviv with the Negev Museum of Art within walking distance.
Tel Tel-aviv is just an easy car ride from the property.
